The Amsterdam City Swim

While our research might make you believe otherwise, we want you to know that this is not a real event. However, it should be!
Image

In 2024, the Paralympics will be hosted in Paris. Before this event, the city of Amsterdam want to organise a similar event in May that utilizes its canal system. The swim will be 5km long.
